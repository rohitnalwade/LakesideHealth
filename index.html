Our primary responsibility is to the well-being of the individual employee. We prioritize the therapeutic relationship above all else, ensuring it remains a safe, non-judgmental space, completely insulated from corporate performance metrics. Our Story & Mission

Lakeside Health was born from a unique intersection of technology, business, and a deep, personal calling to make mental health accessible and affordable.

The "Lakeside" Philosophy

The name "Lakeside" refers to the banks of Powai Lake, next to IIT Bombay, where our founder, Rohit Nalwade, conceived the idea of building ventures that solve real human problems.

A Founder's Journey

Rohit's career began as a successful technology entrepreneur, building and exiting multiple companies in AI and IoT. However, driven by personal experiences with emotional adversity, he made a profound shift. He pursued a Post-Graduation in Counselling Psychology from the prestigious Tata Institute of Social Sciences (TISS), Mumbai, becoming a trained counselling psychologist.
