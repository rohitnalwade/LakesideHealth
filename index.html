Our Story

Re-engineering the Approach to Mental Well-being

The Lakeside Premise

Why is the approach to mental well-being, a critical component of human capital, so often treated as a compliance afterthought rather than a strategic imperative?

On the banks of Powai Lake, a place of serene reflection adjacent to IIT Bombay, this fundamental question was posed. The answer became the premise for Lakeside Health.

We were founded not just to offer a better service, but to implement a fundamentally different model — one that integrates clinical rigor, technological sophistication, and a deep understanding of corporate dynamics.

A Duality of Expertise

This synthesis is embodied by our founder, Rohit Nalwade. His career began in the crucible of technology, building and scaling ventures in AI and IoT. A profound personal journey then led him to TISS, where he trained as a counselling psychologist. Lakeside Health is the culmination of this unique duality — a firm engineered for scale, security, and measurable outcomes, yet delivered with the empathy and trust of a clinical practice.
