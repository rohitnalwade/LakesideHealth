From First Principles: Re-engineering the Approach to Mental Well-being

The Lakeside Premise

Why is the approach to mental well-being, a critical component of human capital, so often treated as a compliance afterthought rather than a strategic imperative?

On the banks of Powai Lake, a place of serene reflection adjacent to the engine of Indian innovation, IIT Bombay, this fundamental question was posed. The answer became the premise for Lakeside Health.

We were founded not just to offer a better service, but to implement a fundamentally different model‚Äîone that integrates clinical rigor, technological sophistication, and a deep understanding of corporate dynamics. Our origin is the synthesis of two seemingly disparate worlds: the structured, systems-thinking of engineering and the empathetic, nuanced understanding of human psychology.

A Duality of Expertise

This synthesis is embodied by our founder, Rohit Nalwade. His career began in the crucible of technology, building and scaling ventures in AI and IoT. This experience instilled a first-principles approach to problem-solving. A profound personal journey then led him to the Tata Institute of Social Sciences (TISS), where he trained as a counselling psychologist. Lakeside Health is the culmination of this unique duality‚Äîa firm engineered for scale, security, and measurable outcomes, yet delivered with the empathy and trust of a clinical practice.
